My Journey

My interest in technology grew naturally as I explored how software can solve real problems. During my early studies, I was always curious about how systems work behind the scenes, and that curiosity quickly turned into a passion for backend development.

Over time, I started experimenting with different technologies—from building small projects in C# and SQL to developing complete web applications using .NET and Node.js. One of my first big steps was working on an eCommerce project during the ITI program, which gave me hands-on experience in creating secure and scalable systems.

At university, I continued pushing my boundaries by diving into OpenGL game development, AR with Unity, and advanced SQL. Each project not only improved my technical skills but also taught me how to think critically and design solutions that are both efficient and practical.

Now, as a Computer Science student and backend developer in the DEPI program, I’m focused on mastering system design, database management, and modern backend frameworks. What excites me most is turning complex problems into elegant solutions and seeing technology make a real difference.
